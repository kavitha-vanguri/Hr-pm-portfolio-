# HR Intern — Blackmont Consulting

Blackmont Consulting is a student-led organisation focused on providing structured international consulting project experience for students and recent graduates.

At Blackmont, teams perform analytical project work to support organisations in improving decision-making, operational performance, and business outcomes.

Our international team collaborates across projects to deliver practical, structured recommendations from initial analysis through implementation planning.

## Key Duties Include
- Conducting the recruitment process for business consultants through LinkedIn and approved channels
- Maintaining an updated Excel record of candidate contacts and status details
- Attending weekly coordination meetings with the Branch Manager

## What We Offer
- Practical recruitment and consulting support experience
- Eligibility for recommendation letters based on performance
- Networking with consulting project teams and managers
- Opportunities for internal role progression
- Flexible working hours compatible with university studies
