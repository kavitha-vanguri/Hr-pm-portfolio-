# Recruitment Workflow (Template)

A standard end-to-end recruitment process, from requisition to offer.

1. **Requisition** — Hiring manager submits role request; HR confirms budget and job description.
2. **Sourcing** — Job posted on relevant boards/LinkedIn; internal referrals and outreach considered.
3. **Screening** — CVs reviewed against role criteria; shortlist created.
4. **Interviews** — Structured interview stages defined (e.g. HR screen → hiring manager → panel).
5. **Assessment** — Any tests, case studies, or work samples reviewed.
6. **Decision** — Feedback consolidated; final candidate selected.
7. **Offer** — Offer issued, negotiated if needed, and accepted.
8. **Handover to Onboarding** — Candidate details passed to onboarding process.

## Notes
- Track time-to-fill and candidate drop-off at each stage to identify bottlenecks.
- Keep all candidates informed of status, even when not progressing.
